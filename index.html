Fun Physics Facts
  • If you traveled at the speed of light, time would stop.
  • A planet's orbit is actually a straight line, space itself
    is curved by gravity.
  • Neutron stars are so dense that a teaspoon of their material
    would weigh more than the entire population of Earth.
  • In a vacuum all objects will fall at the same speed, no matter
    their weights.

Fun Chemistry Facts
  • Iron Oxide (rust) gives Mars it's red color.
  • DNA is flame retardant.
  • Warm water can freeze faster than cold.
  • Glass is technically not a solid, it flows like
    liquid, only very slowly.

Fun Biology Facts
  • Babies have more bones than adults.
  • The body's response to tickling is actually panic.
  • More organisms live on the skin of one human than there
    are humans living on Earth.
  • The average human gut contains 100 trillion bacteria.

Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

Data Science and Artificial Intelligence are two closely related sub-fields of Computer Science that have received alot of attention in recent years. Without Data Science, the massive amounts of information available to us today to drive decision making would be inaccessible. In the same context, without the innovation of Machine Learning and other AI algorithms, these masses of data would be useless. Humans simply cannot process, decipher, and sort that amount of data quickly or efficiently enough to be feasible without the help of efficient computer programs. From Self-driving cars and face-recognizing home-security cameras, to more efficient Google searches and personalized web-browsing, you most likely interact with some form of artificial intelligence every day without even being aware of it.
